Commands
Music:
 Play: Plays audio based on the given keyword(s) or url. [chill play][audio/song/music] [chill play gaming music]
Queue View the queue (audio playing, and what user requested them). [chill queue]

Skip Skips the currently playing song. If there is two users in the channel with the bot, 2 votes are required. [chill skip]

Connect Connects the bot if it is outside of the voice channel. [chill connect]

Leave Disconnects the bot from the voice channel it is in. [chill leave]

Pause Pauses the current audio playing, the bot must be a voice channel with you. [chill pause]

Resume Resumes the current audio playing, the bot must be a voice channel with you. [chill resume]

Moderation:
Kick Kicks the user from the guild [chill kick][user][Reason (optional)]. [chill kick BinaryBoi Test.]

Ban Bans the user from the guild. [chill ban][user][Reason (optional)]. [chill ban BinaryBoi Test.]

Tempmute Mutes the user from chatting on the guild. You must specify time, and have proper permisions. [Reason (optional)] [chill tempmute BinaryBoi 10s Test.]

Misc:
Serverinfo Gives you server information. [chill serverinfo]

Purge Deletes messages in the specific channel. (2-100) [chill purge 10]

Ping Checks the bot's response time to the you! [chill ping]

Help Shows you this help menu. [chill help]

Say Makes the bot say whatever you want! [chill say I am chill]

Chill Makes sure you understand how chill I am! [chill chill]

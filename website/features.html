Commands


Music:
play:
Plays audio based on the given keyword(s) or url. [chill play][audio/song/music] [chill play gaming music]


queue:
View the queue (audio playing, and what user requested them). [chill queue]


skip:
Skips the currently playing song. If there is two users in the channel with the bot, 2 votes are required. [chill skip]


connect:
Connects the bot if it is outside of the voice channel. [chill connect]


leave:
Disconnects the bot from the voice channel it is in. [chill leave]


pause:
Pauses the current audio playing, the bot must be a voice channel with you. [chill pause]


resume:
Resumes the current audio playing, the bot must be a voice channel with you. [chill resume]


Moderation:
kick:
Kicks the user from the guild [chill kick][user][Reason (optional)]. [chill kick BinaryBoi Test.]


ban:
Bans the user from the guild. [chill ban][user][Reason (optional)]. [chill ban BinaryBoi Test.]


tempmute:
Mutes the user from chatting on the guild. You must specify time, and have proper permisions. [Reason (optional)] [chill tempmute BinaryBoi 10s Test.]


Misc:
serverinfo:
Gives you server information. [chill serverinfo]


purge:
Deletes messages in the specific channel. (2-100) [chill purge 10]


ping:
Checks the bot's response time to the you! [chill ping]


help:
Shows you this help menu. [chill help]


say:
Makes the bot say whatever you want! [chill say I am chill]


chill:
Makes sure you understand how chill I am! [chill chill]
